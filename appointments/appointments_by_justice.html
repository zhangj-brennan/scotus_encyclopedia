For terms with two presidents listed, that indicates that a president died or left office and the vice president finished out the remainder of the term.
Elevation: Three Supreme Court justices were elevated from their position as Associate Justice to the position of Chief Justice.
Recess: Recess Appointments occurred when the Senate was on recess and unable to provide the requisite “advice and consent.”
Source: U.S. Senate, Supreme Court Nominations (1789-Present), https://www.senate.gov/legislative/nominations/SupremeCourtNominations1789present.htm; John S. Castellano, A New Look at Recess Appointments to the Federal Judiciary - United States v. Allocco, 12 Cath. U. L. Rev. 29 (1963).
